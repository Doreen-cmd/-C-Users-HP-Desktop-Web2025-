AGRICULTURE BE A TOOL FOR JOB CREATION

Beyond The Cow is dedicated to connecting the unemployed youth of Nyagatare district to modernized agricultural practices and potential employers in the agricultural field. Join us in our mission to revolutionize the future of agriculture by providing valuable experience and opportunities to the youth.

OUR MISSION

Beyond The Cow is committed to modernizing the agricultural sector in Nyagatare district. With a focus on connecting the youth to sustainable farming practices and innovative technology, our mission is to lead the way towards a more efficient and sustainable future for agriculture.

MODERN AGRICULTURE PRACTICES

Our programs are designed to provide the youth with hands-on experience in modern agricultural techniques, promoting sustainability and efficiency in crop production..

AGRICULTURE MACHINERY SOLUTIONS

We offer access to a wide range of agricultural machinery such as tractors, plowers, irrigation systems, and more, enabling farmers to optimize their resources and improve productivity.

EMPLOYER CONNECTIONS

Beyond The Cow facilitates connections between the youth and potential employers in the agricultural field, creating opportunities for valuable work experience and career growth.

EMPOWERING THE YOUTH FOR A SUSTAINABLE FUTURE

From gaining practical knowledge in modern agricultural methods to accessing agricultural machinery and connecting with potential employers, we empower the youth with the tools and opportunities to contribute to a sustainable and productive agricultural sector.
