ABOUT Us

THE BIG 5

We are a youth-focused initiative dedicated to using agriculture as a powerful tool for job creation in Nyagatare District and the Eastern Province of Rwanda. Our work centers on identifying how modern agricultural practices and available resources can be harnessed to empower the unemployed and engage young people in meaningful, income-generating activities. By promoting access to land, training, technology, and financial support, we aim to turn agriculture into a viable and attractive career path. Our goal is to transform farming from a traditional livelihood into a modern engine for employment, innovation, and economic growth within the community.
